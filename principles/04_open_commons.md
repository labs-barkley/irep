# Open Commons

IREP is not a product, not a company, not an asset to be owned. It is offered to the commons: free and open permanently, including for commercial implementers. What is refused is the financialization of the doorway itself - access to employment, like access to education, is a gateway a society cannot equitably price. Enclosure of the standard is out of scope, forever. The initiator is the initial steward - not the owner.
